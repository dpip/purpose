Contract Hound
User-friendly Contract Managment System For Small To Mid Sized Tech Companies
Existing contract management systems suffer from two major problems -- they have a very high price point, and their design is based on legacy systems from the 80s and 90s. Contract Hound was created with the goal of a leaner, more user-friendly system -- one targeted at small to mid-sized tech companies. We wanted all the core features of an enterprise CMS, but one packaged with an interface that was simple and fun.

We settled on a simple organizational system of “contracts” being pinned to “boards” so that users could be in charge of their files. A robust search feature would help users find particular contracts.
First Iteration
Challenge: Getting the Right Data
We quickly encountered a major problem. Technical constraints barred us from being able to reliably read the contracts uploaded to the system. As the rest of the design matured, we hit on our solution -- crowdsourcing.
Second Iteration
We adjusted the contract screen to make the details pane more prominent, and included reminders within that panel. We took a hard look at why people were opening that app and simplified the left nav to focus on those use cases.
Final Iteration

In our final iteration, we build out user management and account upgrade screens, added preferences for notifications and other profile customization, and created admin interactions for suspending or deleting users.
Results
Contract Hound is now live and available for use. It’s a great way to easily store, organize, & configure alerts for your company’s contracts.
